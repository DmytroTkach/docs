**To resend the email with the generated one-time password, do the following:**

1. Log in to the Management Tool as a user with the [Client configuration management permission](https://documentation.ekransystem.com/display/DOC/Client+Permissions).

2. Click the **Access Management** navigation link to the left.

3. On the **Access Management** page, open the **One-time Passwords** tab.

4. On the **One-Time Passwords** tab, click the **Resend Email** link for the target one-time password.

5. In the confirmation message, click **OK**.

6. A new one-time password is generated and sent to the userâ€™s email address.

**NOTE: You can resend the emails with one-time passwords with the Generated & Sent or Sending Failed states only.**
