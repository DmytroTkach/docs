It is recommended to use Ekran System primarily to monitor subcontractors that maintain your servers.

If you are want to monitor the activities of your employees in the workplace, it is possible to configure Ekran System according to Article 29 Working Party recommendations and your corporate policy.

For more information, please refer to the [Resources](https://www.ekransystem.com/en/resources#white-papers) page on the Ekran System website.
