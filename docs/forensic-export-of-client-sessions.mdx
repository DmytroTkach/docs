**To perform a forensic export of all filtered Client sessions**, click the Â icon next to the **Client Sessions** tab on the right, and then select **Export Filtered Sessions** in the drop-down list, and select **Forensic Export**.

In the **Sessions Forensic Export** pop-up window that opens, click **Export**.

If you want to protect exported sessions with a password, select the **Protect with password** option, and enter the password in the **Password** and **Confirm password** fields, and then click **Export**. The **Forensic Export History** page then opens, displaying the export progress.

As soon as the export process finishes, the resulting files are available for downloading on the **Forensic Export History** page that opens. Click the **Download** icon to download the required file with the results of the **Forensic Export**.

To play the exported sessions, download the **Ekran Forensic Player** via the link at the top of the **Forensic Export History** page.
